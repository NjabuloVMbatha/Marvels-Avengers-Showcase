About Our Heroes

Let us dive into our childhood legends of the Marvel Avengers. Here is a character brief overview of some of your superheroes .

Thor

Thor Odinson

The mighty God of Thunder, prince of Asgard, and a founding member of the Avengers. Wielding his enchanted hammer Mjolnir (or Stormbreaker), Thor protects both his home realm and Earth from cosmic threats. His immense strength, control over lightning, and ability to fly make him a formidable force.

Iron Man

Anthony "Tony" Stark

Genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist. Tony Stark is a brilliant inventor and industrialist who built advanced suits of armor to fight evil. As Iron Man, he constantly innovates and often provides the technological backbone for the Avengers, defending the world with his wit and advanced weaponry.
